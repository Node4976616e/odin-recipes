Ingredients

  • 500g Ground/Minced pork
  • eidam cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Ground cumin
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • plain flour
  • breadcrumbs
  • oil/lard

Steps

  1. Shred the eidam cheese and add it to minced meat
  2. Add salt,black pepper and ground cumin.
  3. Mix all properly
  4. Take a small portion of the mix and make a small patty in your hands
  5. Mix eggs with milk
  6. Cover schnitzels in flour, egg and breadcrumbs exactly in this order
  7. Fry the schnitzels in oil or lard or other grease for frying
  8. For a side you can make american potatoes, boiled potatos, mashed potatoes or honestly anything you like
  9. Enjoy!
