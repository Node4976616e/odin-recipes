Ingredients

  • 500g Ground/Minced Beef
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix 2 Eggs and Ground Beef in bowl
  2. Add all seasoning into mix
  3. Top it with shredded cheese
  4. Put in a casserole and bake it for 35 minutes at 180 degrees celsium.
