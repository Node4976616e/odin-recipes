Ingredients

  • 500g Ground/Minced Beef
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Rice
  • Fermented Sour Cabbage

Steps

  1. Sauter minced beef on a deep pan until it is almost done
  2. Add water and cabbage
  3. Season it with black pepper, salt and paprika
  4. Add rice and cook until the rice is ready
  5. When it is done you can top it with sour cream
